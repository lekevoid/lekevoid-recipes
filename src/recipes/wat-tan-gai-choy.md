1. P﻿repare a slurry with the corn starch and beat the egg in a bowl.
2. C﻿ut the gai choy stems, bite-size. Separate the stems from the leaves.
3. B﻿lanch the stems for 1 minute. After the 1 minutes, add the leaves to the boiling water and let everything blanch for 1 more minute. Then take away and drain.
4. P﻿ut some oil in a pan or wok. Add garlic. Fry until garlic starts changing color.
5. A﻿dd gai choy. Cook for a minute.
6. A﻿dd the slurry and the egg. Cook a little to let it thicken and glaze.
7. A﻿dd the soy sauce and sesame oil.
8. R﻿eady to serve.
